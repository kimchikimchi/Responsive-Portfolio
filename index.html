About Me

My name is Bikini the Cat. Born in 2010 on a sunny day in Atlanta, GA, I am trice divorced mother to four lovely children named Furball, Coco, Tigger and Munchikin (pics are in my 'Portfolio' section. Children are mother's greatest hits. Aren't they? ;-)) As you can tell from my profile picture, I've led a rather storied life with some very proud moments and others not so much. In my wildest youth, I experimented with bootleg milk and catnips and partied with many feline models and rock-n-rollers in Nu Yok City.

I am now happily retired from pet modeling and trying to lead a healthier life style away from spotlights and sins. If I had a wish--which isn't so much in my humble opinion, I am currently seeking an available bachelor cat to spend my golden years with. Please--no strays or rabid cats. Have a blessed day.
